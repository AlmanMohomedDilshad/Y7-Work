About Sri Lanka

Welcome to Sri Lanka, a tropical paradise known for its rich history, diverse culture, and breathtaking landscapes. Explore ancient temples, relax on pristine beaches, and indulge in the warmth of Sri Lankan hospitality.

Top Destinations

Sigiriya

Discover the majestic Sigiriya Rock, a UNESCO World Heritage Site offering panoramic views of the surrounding landscape.

Kandy

Immerse yourself in the cultural heart of Sri Lanka. Visit the Temple of the Tooth Relic and experience traditional Kandyan dance performances.

Activities

Wildlife Safari

Embark on an exciting wildlife safari and witness diverse nature and creatures that roam the island of Sri Lanka, including elephants, leopards, and exotic bird species.

Beach Relaxation

Unwind on the golden beaches of Sri Lanka. Whether you prefer surfing, snorkeling, or simply sunbathing, there's only one rule and that is to have fun.

Travel Tips

  • Respect local customs and traditions
  • Try traditional Sri Lankan dishes like rice and curry
  • Carry sunscreen and stay hydrated, especially in tropical climates
  • Try and learn a few phrases in Sri Lanka's language Sinhala to get the best response back from the locals
