Sri Lanka

Sri lanka is an island of the south cost of India separated by the Palk Straight. Sri Lanka was a few decades ago called Ceylon. Sri Lanka has a population of 22.16 Milion!
Sri Lanka also has beautiful nature with a variety of different animals. Some of the many animals in Sri Lanka are Monkey's, elephants, and leopards.

The Terrific Train Ride
